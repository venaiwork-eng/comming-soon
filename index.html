How it works

From Raw Data to Funded Deals in 3 Steps

Connect your operational data, let VenAI synthesize and route verified opportunities, then execute and monitor — with zero blind spots from first meeting to post-investment.

Step 01

Connect (Zero Friction)

Founders securely connect their operational APIs (Stripe, Xero, Plaid) in minutes. Our engine ingests the raw data without human interference, ensuring 100% cryptographic accuracy.

Step 02

Synthesize & Route (Zero Noise)

VenAI's custom algorithms translate the raw data into an institutional-grade Scorecard, evaluating unit economics and burn rate. The system then automatically pushes the startup to the specific VC dashboards that match those exact metrics.

Step 03

Execute & Monitor (Zero Blind Spots)

VCs review the pre-verified data, initiate contact, and execute the deal faster. Post-funding, the API connection remains open for automated, real-time portfolio health tracking.
