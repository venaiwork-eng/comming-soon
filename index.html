Deal Desk

Legal Language Translator

When deals approach signature, the term sheet, SHA and SPA arrive in dense legal language. The Deal Desk decomposes every clause into plain English and flags the commercial mechanics — including the gotchas a casual read will miss.

Clause 7.2 Founder-adverse
2× participating liquidation preference
→ On exit, the fund collects 2× their cheque off the top, then shares the remainder pro rata. On a £4M exit of a £2M raise, founders receive ~£0.
Clause 11.4 Standard
Pro-rata participation rights
→ The investor reserves the right (not obligation) to maintain their ownership percentage by participating in your next round. Market standard.
